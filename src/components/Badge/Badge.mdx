# Badge

<Meta of={BadgeStories} />

Provides additional content to an element by generating a small badge to the top-right of its child(ren).

If a badge's child has text associated with it, the badge is considered presentational; presentational elements do not require labels as they have no semantic importance to assistive technology. Icons used within badges are scaled to 0.625x.
